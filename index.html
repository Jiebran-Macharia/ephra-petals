About Ephra Petals

From Nyeri County farms to Kenya’s leading floral clients

Ephra Petals is rooted in Nyeri County, Kenya, where we partner with family farms and manage our own growing fields. Our business brings fresh specialty flowers to florists, event designers, exporters and decorative buyers who expect consistent quality and reliable service.

We work directly with local flower farmers, providing stable demand, fair pricing and training in careful harvesting. Every stem is selected for freshness, durability and presentation before we prepare it for local delivery or export.

Our Flowers

Premium Ammi and Scabiosa varieties from farm-direct sources

Our selection highlights three signature flower types that blend natural texture, strong vase life and floral elegance for premium bouquets and decor.

Ammi

Queen Anne’s Lace / Bishop’s Weed

Appearance: Large flat-topped umbels of tiny white flowers resembling delicate lace.

Role in bouquets: A volume filler that adds a soft, natural, field-picked look.

Best uses: Wedding bouquets, event installations, delicate vase arrangements.

Unique selling point: Provides airiness and a premium farm-fresh texture while supporting local growers.

Scabiosa

Pincushion Flower

Appearance: Ruffled petals surround a charming pincushion center with soft, elegant texture.

Role in bouquets: Works as a focal or secondary bloom to add movement and graceful structure.

Best uses: Bridal arrangements, high-end bouquets, decorative installations.

Unique selling point: Its wavy stems and refined look bring sophistication and depth to floral designs.

Scabiosa ‘Butterfly Blue’

Soft blue-lavender pollinator-friendly bloom

Appearance: Compact blue-lavender flowers with soft stems and glossy leaves.

Role in bouquets: Adds delicate color, texture and natural charm to premium collections.

Best uses: Natural bouquets, pollinator-friendly arrangements, garden-inspired decor.

Unique selling point: Attracts bees and butterflies, making it ideal for eco-conscious farms and floral designs.

Farm Sourcing

Farm-direct sourcing for fresher flowers and stronger farmer partnerships

We source flowers directly from trusted Nyeri County farms and our own growing fields. This direct route reduces handling time, improves freshness and gives local flower farmers a reliable market for premium stems.

  • Direct grower relationships that cut out middlemen
  • Harvesting flowers at peak condition for better vase life
  • Bunching, quality checking and careful packaging before dispatch
  • Pollinator-friendly practices and sustainable farm care

Our sourcing process is designed for florists, event planners and exporters who need beautifully consistent, farm-fresh flowers from Kenya’s premium flower region.
